1. Topic : - Sales Dashboard – Mobile Sales Analytics (Power BI) 

2. Short Description

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze mobile phone sales performance across brands, cities, age groups, payment methods, and monthly trends.The purpose of this dashboard is to help stakeholders understand sales patterns, identify high-value customer segments, and support data-driven decision-making using clear visual insights.

3. Techonlogy
   Power BI Desktop : Used to design the dashboard, create visuals, build relationships, and publish the final report.
It provides the interactive canvas for building high-quality business intelligence solutions.

Power Query was used for:

Data cleaning, Removing duplicates,Transforming columns, Changing data types, Creating calculated columns before loading to the model
This ensures the dataset is accurate and analysis-ready.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

DAX measures were used for: Total Sales, Total Quantity, Average Price, KPI metrics, grouping of age and rating

Data Modeling

A star-schema-style model was applied (fact table with dimension fields).
Relationships were defined to support filtering through slicers (Brand, Age Group, Payment Method, Year, Month, Quarter, etc.).
This improves performance and ensures accurate cross-filtering across visuals.

4. Dashboard Highlights & Key Insights
Overall Performance

Total Sales: 769M

Total Transactions: 3,835, Total Quantity Sold: 19,150, Average Price per Unit: 40.11K
Healthy revenue with strong sales volume and a high average selling price

Brand Performance
Apple is the top-selling brand, Samsung, Oppo, Vivo, and Xiaomi follow.
Shows strong demand for premium devices.

Customer Ratingsx`
Majority ratings: Good (2,331) and Average (1,195). Very few Poor ratings
Indicates positive customer experience with scope for improvement.

Payment Method Insights
UPI is the most used payment method, followed by Debit and Credit Card.
Cash usage is minimal, highlighting digital adoption.

Age Group Contribution
41–45 years age group generates the highest sales. 26–40 years also contribute significantly.
This reflects stronger purchasing power among mature customers.

Price vs Sales Analysis
Higher sales quantities are associated with higher price categories. Total sales rise sharply as units sold increase.
Shows strong demand for mid-to-premium models.
